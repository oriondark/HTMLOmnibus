Block & Chain

As the complexities of navigating healthcare data continues to grow, we need one thing more than anything else. We need to agree. We need to publicly decide how our data should be handled and stored. We need to get together and make it easier for patients to understand the data, easier for physicians to make informed decisions, make it easier to report on, act on, and grow in our ever evolving knowledge of patient care and the data complexities that surround us.

Enter the blockchain. Or distributed ledger. You can read all about its uses these days. The ledger is being targeted for use in a wide variety of industries. It certainly has application here. So, what is its use, and how best can we leverage this rapidly growing techology? First, we start by learning what it can do. Then we talk to others who have the same interests we do, a better path to patient care, reconciliation, compliance, and adherence. To that end, the Distributed HealthCare Ledger Alliance is gathering members to start this very important conversation. Interested in joining? Send us your contact information. Our goal is to bring healthcare knowledge together with technology with those who have a vested interest in all outcomes.
